Important symbols for the formal math model of diffusion.
Symbol Description Values
$p_S(t)$ Prevalence of the *Sustainable* practice $[0, 1]$
$f_S$ Fitness of the *Sustainable* practice $[0, \infty)$
$\alpha$ Adoption rate for contagion transmission $[0, 1]$
$\delta$ Drop rate for contagion transmission $[0, 1]$
$S$ Success rate: frequency of successful simulated interventions $[0, 1]$
